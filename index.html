Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
  • Click 4 random points on the canvas to create a Cardinal Spline between your 2nd and 3rd points!
  • Continue clicking around to create more and more fun curves
  • Control the "tightness" of your curves using the Tension buttons
  • Click "Toggle render mode" after making a curve to have a (rotating) object traverse its path
  • You can also pause, speed up, or slow down the object
  • Have fun! :)
